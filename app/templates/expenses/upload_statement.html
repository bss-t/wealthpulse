Supported Statement Formats

The parser can extract transactions from statements that include:

  • Date: DD/MM/YYYY, DD-MM-YYYY, YYYY-MM-DD, DD-MMM-YYYY formats
  • Description: Transaction merchant/description text
  • Amount: Numeric values with optional commas (e.g., 1,234.56)

Tip: After importing, review the transactions list and update categories for more accurate expense tracking.
